<original_problem_statement>
The user, Aman Agarwal, wants to build a production-grade, private mobile app for his jewelry business, Yash Trade. The app is intended for his existing network of ~40,000 wholesale and retail jewelers. The primary goal is to create a daily-use business utility that increases customer retention and repeat business.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**

*   **Core App Experience:**
    *   An endless, Instagram-style feed of jewelry photos and videos (silver, gold, diamond).
    *   A prominent daily rates module for Gold and Silver.
    *   A robust rewards/loyalty points system.
    *   Functionality to Request Call and Request Video Call.
    *   A Silver Jewellery Calculator for quick estimates.
    *   An AI-powered assistant for selling tips and product knowledge.
    *   A customer portal to view account details, order status, and rewards.
    *   Stories/highlights for daily updates.

*   **User Roles & Access:**
    *   **Admin:** Full control over content, users, rates, rewards, and analytics via an admin panel.
    *   **Executive:** A separate, limited-access panel for support/video call executives to manage customer requests.
    *   **Jeweller (User):** Private access via OTP-verified phone numbers.

*   **Key Technical & UX Decisions:**
    *   **Authentication:** JWT-based with a mocked phone OTP.
    *   **AI:** Claude Sonnet 4.5, using the Emergent LLM Key.
    *   **Design:** A premium, dark theme with gold accents, emphasizing a professional, luxury feel.
    *   **Internationalization:** Support for English, Hindi, and Punjabi.
    *   **Content Strategy:** The app must support a massive library of 10,000+ images, many without detailed descriptions, functioning as a private media viewer.
</original_problem_statement>
